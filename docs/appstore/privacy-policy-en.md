# Privacy Policy (App Store & Google Play)

**Last Updated: May 29, 2026**

The non-profit organization **TUTODECODE** (SIREN: 102 763 133) publishes the **T2DECODE** application. This privacy policy explains our approach to protecting your personal data when you download and use our mobile and desktop application.

T2DECODE was built according to the strictest standards of *Privacy by Design*.

## 1. Data Collected

**We collect absolutely no personal data.**

The T2DECODE application is designed to be completely offline ("Offline-First").
- No user account is required to use the application.
- No contact information (name, email, phone number) is requested.
- We do not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising trackers, or tracking SDKs.

## 2. Local Storage and Data Processing

All information generated during your use of the application remains **strictly stored on your local device**:
- Your course progress and scores.
- Queries and conversation histories sent to the Artificial Intelligence tutor (Ghost AI). The LLM module works 100% locally without invoking any external API (Zero Cloud Policy).
- Your app settings and preferences.

The TUTODECODE organization has no technical or physical access to this data.

## 3. Permissions and Network Access

The application may request certain system permissions necessary exclusively for the operation of specific tools:
- **Local Network (LAN):** Required only if you use the "Ghost Link" tool (peer-to-peer chat). The transmitted data is end-to-end encrypted and does not pass through any of the organization's servers.
- **Storage Access:** Required only if you decide to export your notebooks or import external course modules.

These permissions are never used to exfiltrate your data.

## 4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Since we do not collect any data, **we do not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties.**

## 5. Application Updates

When the application checks for updates to its "Cheat Sheets" or course modules, it connects directly to TUTODECODE's public GitHub repositories. During this action, your IP address may be visible to GitHub's servers, in accordance with GitHub's privacy policy. The TUTODECODE organization does not store or process these connection logs.

## 6. Children's Privacy Law (COPPA / GDPR Kids)

Our application is an educational tool intended for all audiences, including children. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted over the internet, the application is inherently compliant with international laws on children's online privacy (notably COPPA in the United States and GDPR in Europe).

## 7. Your Rights

In accordance with the European GDPR and other global privacy laws, you have full control over your data. Since all your data is stored on your device, you can exercise your right to be forgotten and deletion by:
1. Clearing the application's cache and data from your phone/computer settings.
2. Simply uninstalling the application.
